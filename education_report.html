K-12 Districts Have the Lowest Email Security Gateway Adoption in the Public Sector

Education email security analysis: 18,476 K-12 districts and 3,246 higher education institutions across 51 jurisdictions

  • 18,476 K-12 districts in the national registry — the second-largest public entity category
  • Only 8.2% of K-12 domains use an email security gateway — lowest of any entity type (counties: 25.9%, higher ed: 20.4%)
  • K-12 DMARC averages 46.7% nationally — wide state variation from 67.5% (OR) to 7.7% (WV)
  • Higher education leads all entity types with 84.4% DMARC and 20.4% gateway adoption
  • Texas K-12 outperforms: 67.2% DMARC vs 46.7% national average (+20.5pp)
