Why this matters for education research:

K-12 districts are prime targets for phishing, ransomware, and business email compromise. DMARC enforcement prevents domain spoofing — yet fewer than half of K-12 districts nationwide have it configured. The data reveals massive state-by-state variation (10.8% to 67.3%), suggesting policy and funding interventions can move the needle.
